Suggested Itineraries

  • Golden Triangle (6 days) — Delhi → Agra → Jaipur. Culture, forts, Taj Mahal.
  • Kerala Backwaters (5 days) — Kochi → Alleppey → Munnar. Houseboats, tea gardens, Ayurveda.
  • Himalayan Escape (7-9 days) — Rishikesh → Haridwar → Rishikesh/Rafting or trekking in Uttarakhand.
  • Goa Sun & Sea (4 days) — Beaches, water-sports, Portuguese heritage.

Quick Tips

  1. Best time: Oct–Mar for most regions.
  2. Local SIM & digital payments are widely available.
  3. Respect local customs and dress modestly at religious sites.
  4. Book Taj Mahal tickets and permits in advance for peak season.
